[method] —
HodgkinHuxley()

This model is used to calculate the membrane potential of a neuron. The calculation is based on sodium ion flow, potassium ion flow and leakage ion flow. (Hodgkin, A. L. and Huxley, A. F. (1952) "A Quantitative Description of Membrane Current and its Application to Conduction and Excitation in Nerve" Journal of Physiology 117: 500-544)
